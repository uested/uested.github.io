Most Predictive Features for GNI in country income groups:

  • High Income
    • 1. Ratio of young literate females to males (% ages 15-24)
    • 2. Literacy rate, youth male (% of males ages 15-24)
    • 3. Literacy rate, youth total (% of people ages 15-24)
    • 4. Literacy rate, adult male (% of males ages 15 and above)
  • Upper-Middle Income
    • 1. Ratio of school attendance of orphans to school attendance of non-orphans ages 10-14
    • 2. School enrollment, tertiary (% gross)
    • 3. School enrollment, tertiary, female (% gross)
    • 4. Literacy rate, adult male (% of males ages 15 and above)
  • Lower-Middle Income
    • 1. Literacy rate, youth male (% of males ages 15-24)
    • 2. Literacy rate, youth total (% of people ages 15-24)
    • 3. Public spending on education, total (% of GDP)
    • 4. School enrollment, secondary, female (% gross)
  • Lower Income
    • 1. School enrollment, tertiary (% gross)
    • 2. School enrollment, tertiary, female (% gross)
    • 3. School enrollment, secondary (% gross)
    • 4. School enrollment, secondary, male (% gross)
